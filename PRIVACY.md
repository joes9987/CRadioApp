# Privacy Policy for Church 668 Radio

**Last updated: February 13, 2025**

## About the App

Church 668 Radio is a mobile application for The Church of the First Born Assembly Miracle Center. The app streams THE OLD TIME GOSPEL HOUR FAMILY RADIO and provides links to connect with the church via social media and the church website.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information through this app.**

The app functions entirely on your device. No account creation or login is required. We do not use analytics, advertising, or any other services that would gather user data.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function:

- **Internet** — Required to stream radio audio
- **Wake lock** — Keeps the app active during playback so streaming continues
- **Foreground service** — Allows radio playback to continue when the app is in the background or the screen is locked
- **Battery optimization exemption** — Optional; requested so radio playback is not interrupted by power-saving features when the screen is off

## Third-Party Links

The app includes links that open external apps or websites:

- Facebook
- WhatsApp
- YouTube
- X (Twitter)
- The church website (churchofthefirstbornassembly.org)

When you tap these links, you leave our app. The privacy practices of those third-party services apply to any data you provide or they collect. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

## Audio Streaming

Radio audio is streamed from THE OLD TIME GOSPEL HOUR FAMILY RADIO via a third-party streaming service. The stream is played directly on your device. We do not record, log, or transmit any information about your listening.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app does not require or collect any personal information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the Church 668 Radio app, please contact The Church of the First Born Assembly Miracle Center through the contact information on the church website.
